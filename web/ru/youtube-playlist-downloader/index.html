FAQ

Is there a maximum playlist length?

No hard cap, but very large playlists (500+ videos) may take several minutes to process in full.

Are playlists downloaded in order?

Yes. Оригиналal playlist ordering is preserved by default.

Is there a limit to the number of videos in a playlist?

We can theoretically parse playlists of any size, but for optimal performance and browser stability, we recommend downloading playlists with 200 videos or fewer at a time.

Will it download as one giant file or separate files?

Each video in the playlist is converted and downloaded as its own separate file. We do not merge them into a single massive audio/video file.

Can I download a 'Watch Later' playlist?

Your 'Watch Later' playlist is private to your account. Our servers cannot access it. You would need to move those videos to a Public or Unlisted playlist first.

What happens if the playlist contains a live stream?

If the live stream has ended and is available as a VOD, it will download normally. If the stream is currently live, the tool will skip it, as it has no finite length.

How long does a 50-video playlist take to convert?

Parsing the list takes only seconds. The conversion time depends on the total length of the videos and the chosen quality, but our concurrent processing usually finishes a typical music playlist in under 3 minutes.

Do I have to click download 50 times?

Currently, browser security restrictions prevent websites from automatically triggering 50 file downloads at once. You will need to click the download button for each generated file.

Does it download the playlist thumbnail?

The individual video files will have their respective video thumbnails embedded as metadata (for audio formats) or as the poster frame (for video formats).

Can I download a channel's entire video list?

If the channel has a 'Play All' button or a playlist containing all their uploads, yes. Just copy that specific playlist URL.

Are the files numbered?

The files retain their original YouTube titles. If the creator numbered the titles (e.g., 'Episode 1: ...'), that numbering will be in your downloaded file.

Why did only half the videos appear?

This usually means the missing videos are either Private, Deleted, or Region-Blocked. Our tool can only fetch publicly accessible media.
