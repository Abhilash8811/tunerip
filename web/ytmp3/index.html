YTMP3 Audio Quality & Format Options

MP3 Bitrate Selection

  • 128 kbps - Standard quality, approximately 1MB per minute, ideal for spoken content and podcasts
  • 192 kbps - Enhanced quality, good balance between file size and audio fidelity for casual listening
  • 256 kbps - Premium quality, excellent for music with noticeable improvement in clarity and depth
  • 320 kbps - Maximum MP3 quality, indistinguishable from CD audio for most listeners, recommended for audiophiles

Advanced Audio Formats

  • M4A (AAC) - Modern codec with superior compression efficiency, smaller files with better quality than MP3
  • WAV - Uncompressed lossless format, perfect for professional audio work and archival purposes
  • OGG Vorbis - Open-source alternative offering excellent quality-to-size ratio
  • OPUS - Cutting-edge codec optimized for internet streaming, best quality at low bitrates
  • FLAC - Lossless compression maintaining 100% original quality while reducing file size
