YouTube to M4A Converter

Export YouTube audio as M4A (AAC) — the most efficient audio format for Apple devices.

M4A = AAC in an MP4 container

M4A is the native audio format used by Apple Music and the iTunes Store. At any given bitrate, AAC sounds noticeably better than MP3, especially at lower rates like 128 kbps. If you're listening on iPhone, iPad, Mac, or HomePod, M4A is the smarter default.

Direct mux where possible

YouTube's native audio stream is already AAC for most videos. When that's the case, we simply re-mux the AAC bitstream into an M4A container without re-encoding — meaning zero generation loss and near-instant processing.

FAQ

Can Android play M4A?

Yes. Android has supported M4A/AAC natively since version 3.

Does M4A include album art?

We embed the video thumbnail as cover art whenever the source provides one.
