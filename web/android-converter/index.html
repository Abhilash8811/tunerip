Download YouTube on Android without an APK

Google blocks most converter APKs from the Play Store for obvious reasons, and side-loading a random APK from the internet is a security risk. yt2mp3.lol lets you convert YouTube links to MP3 or MP4 entirely in Chrome, Samsung Internet, or any modern Android browser — no APK, no root required, nothing to give permissions to beyond Downloads.

Step-by-step on Android

  1. Open YouTube, tap Share → Copy link.
  2. Switch to Chrome and open yt2mp3.lol.
  3. Tap the URL field and paste.
  4. Pick MP3 or MP4 and the quality you want.
  5. Tap Convert. When the download button appears, tap it.
  6. The file lands in Downloads and appears in your gallery or music player.

Install as a PWA for one-tap access

On the Install page we offer a one-tap "Add to Home Screen" shortcut that pins yt2mp3.lol to your launcher like a regular app. It loads offline-capable assets, opens in its own window (no browser chrome), and weighs less than 100 KB.

Samsung, OnePlus, Xiaomi and other OEMs

Because we rely on standard web APIs (Fetch, Blob, Download Attribute), we work identically on every major Android skin — One UI, OxygenOS, MIUI, ColorOS, stock Pixel. The only OEM-specific thing is where the file lands (usually Download/, sometimes Downloads/).

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need root?

No. Chrome's standard download API handles everything.

Why aren't there YouTube-to-MP3 apps on the Play Store?

Google's policy blocks most of them. The web-based approach sidesteps that entirely.

Where do downloads go?

Your Downloads folder. Most Android music players and galleries index that folder automatically.

Can I install yt2mp3.lol as an app?

Yes — tap the three-dot menu in Chrome and pick 'Add to Home screen' (the prompt appears automatically on our Install page).

Will it work on a tablet?

Yes — the interface scales up to Android tablets and Chromebooks.
