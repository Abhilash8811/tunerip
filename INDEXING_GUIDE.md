## Quick Indexing Steps

### 1. Request Indexing in Google Search Console
For each new page:
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Use the URL Inspection tool (top search bar)
3. Enter the full URL (e.g., `https://yt2mp3.lol/youtube-audio-download-mp3/`)
4. Click "Request Indexing"
5. Wait 1-2 days for Google to crawl

**Priority URLs to index:**
- https://yt2mp3.lol/youtube-audio-download-mp3/
